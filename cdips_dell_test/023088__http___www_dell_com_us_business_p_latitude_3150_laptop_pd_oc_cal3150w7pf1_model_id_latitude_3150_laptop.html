Fully equipped for learning
Built for success: Choose between Intel® Celeron® or Intel® Pentium® processors and Windows 7 Professional or Windows 8.1 for high-performance multitasking. An 11.6-inch anti-glare display, full-size keyboard and 180-degree hinge encourage student productivity, creativity and classroom collaboration.

Enable anywhere, anytime learning: With seamless access to documents and thousands of education apps even without an Internet connection, students can stay productive wherever they are.

Connectivity alerts: Observe student activity with a network activity light that notifies educators when students are online.

All-day learning power: Excellent battery life up to 10 hours*, keeps students up and running anywhere, anytime. The optional mobile computing cart stores and charges laptops overnight, so your systems are always ready for class.

Interactive learning environment: With two speakers, an HD webcam and dual array microphones, students can watch videos and develop multimedia presentations together.
Return to Top
Unmatched durability
Spill-proof design: For peace of mind even during the messiest projects, Latitude 11 Education Series is the only student laptop with a fully-sealed keyboard and touch pad that provides best-in-class protection against liquid spills.

Built tough: The rubberized LCD and base trim absorbs shock for superior drop protection. The solid state drive is designed without spinning parts, so it can withstand accidental drops and bumps.

Extensively tested: Latitude 11 is MIL-STD (U.S. Military Standard) tested for durability, pressure, temperature, humidity, shock and vibration so it can handle student life.
