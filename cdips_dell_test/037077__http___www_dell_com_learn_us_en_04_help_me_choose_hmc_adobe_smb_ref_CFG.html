What is Acrobat Standard DC?
Acrobat DC is the completely reimagined desktop version of the world’s best PDF solution. It includes, with Adobe Document Cloud, a mobile app so you can fill, sign and share PDFs on any device. Adobe Photoshop Elements 14 & Adobe Premiere Elements 14
Adobe Photoshop Elements 14 & Adobe Premiere Elements 14 - Capture the moment now. Perfect the photo or video later.

  • Smartphone selfies. Tablet scenes. Posed portraits and action-cam footage. You take more photos and videos than ever before. Keep focusing on the memories—Adobe Photoshop Elements 14 & Premiere Elements 14 will make them look great with quick edits, stylish effects, artistic touches, and fun transformations. Create beautiful scrapbook pages and cards. Share to favorite social sites, and view on 4K and HD TVs. And with all your photos and videos at your fingertips, you can relive any memory at any moment.
Key features Of Adobe Photoshop Elements 14 & Adobe Premiere Elements 14

Guided Edits — Get step-by-step guidance to improve your photos and videos. Create drama by popping one or more colors in a video scene. Add a sense of motion to your action photos. Create slow-motion effects for your movie. And resize photos in seconds.

Tell flashy stories with motion titles — Make a splash from the start of your movie by easily adding titles that bounce, spin, or zoom across the screen.

Custom looks for any photo — Elements analyzes the subject, color, and lighting and gives you five effects that fit perfectly.

Go beyond HD with 4K — Edit and view high-resolution 4K movies for the ultimate cinematic experience.

Sound your best — Audio sets your movie’s mood—and now it’s easier than ever to fine-tune your sound.

Quick photo edits made easier — Rotate, resize, and make quick fixes even faster in a more friendly and easy-to-use Quick Edit mode.

Easily export in multiple formats — Export optimized movies in formats for viewing on any device or screen, from mobile phones to big-screen 4K and HD TVs. Create for traditional television (4:3) or widescreen (16:9) format. And export movies in formats that look great on mobile devices like the iPad, iPod, iPhone, and other smartphones.

Find by people and places, faster — The Elements Organizer makes it easier than ever to find photos and videos based on the people in them, what you were doing, and where they were taken.
