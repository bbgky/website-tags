Give your network a boost
Support wire-speed performance and help provide fast access to data locally and across networks with Dell Networking 6200 series gigabit Ethernet (GbE) switches.
• Support up to 4GbE connections per switch and up to 576 connections per stack of 12 systems.
• Help provide fast 10GbE connectivity directly to servers, routers, enterprise backbones and data centers with up to four 10Gb fiber (SFP+) ports and up to two 10GBase-T copper Ethernet uplinks.
• Connect at up to 10 times the speed of 10/100 networks, and help get the most out of your PC and server performance by matching your NIC speeds.
Help reduce network congestion
Efficiently connect your networks and manage traffic with support for Layer 3 routing and multicast protocols.
• Provide core-switching capabilities and connect your different networks and inter-virtual LAN traffic.
• Route your network traffic using a wide range of industry-standard LAN-routing protocols.
• Help increase your number of unique IP addresses and improve scalability with IP version 6 (IPv6) support.

Easily expand with high-density stacking
Grow your network as needed and help provide redundancy by stacking up to 12 Dell Networking 6200 series switches. Make the most of your expansion efforts with simplified deployment and configuration.
Help ensure quality of service
Prioritize your critical network traffic and optimize voice over IP (VoIP) performance with integrated quality of service (QoS) features.
• Gain improved control over network performance and make sure that time-critical traffic is prioritized.
• Help provide an ideal solution for unified communications, wireless access points, virtual desktops and Internet SCSI (iSCSI) storage.
• Implement integrated voice virtual LANs (VLANs) for VoIP applications.

Employ enterprise-level management and security
Draw from a deep and rich set of management and security capabilities with Dell Networking 6200 series switches.
• Perform advanced administration on your Dell Networking switches using a familiar and intuitive command-line interface (CLI) or an easy-to-use, web-based graphical user interface (GUI).
• Monitor network traffic using sFlow or Simple Network Management Protocol (SNMP) capabilities.
• Help ensure network security with features such as access control lists (ACLs), 802.1x and denial-of-service (DoS) attack prevention.

Dell Networking 6224 Switch
The Dell Networking 6224 is a high-performance Layer 3 switch that offers enhanced distribution for medium to large-sized networks or core switching for small networks. It features 24 RJ-45 ports, 4 combo small form-factor pluggable (SFP) ports and two rear-panel bays with support for optional 10Gb XFP, CX4, 10GBase-T (bay 2) or stacking modules (bay 1). It is ideal for uplinks to a 10Gb core or for direct connections to 10Gb servers or storage.
Dell Networking 6224F Switch
The Dell Networking 6224F is ideal for long-haul Layer 3 connectivity. It combines advanced distribution or core switching capabilities with 24 SFP ports, 4 combo RJ-45 ports and two rear-panel bays with support for optional 10Gb XFP, CX4, 10GBase-T (bay 2) or stacking modules (bay 1). It is ideal for uplinks to a 10Gb core or for direct connections to 10Gb servers or storage.
Dell Networking 6224P Switch
The Dell Networking 6224P is ideal for Layer 3 voice over IP (VoIP) or wireless access point connectivity. It offers comprehensive features, high performance and power over Ethernet (PoE) convenience for end-user networks. It features 24 RJ-45 PoE ports, 4 combo SFP ports and two rear-panel bays with support for optional 10Gb XFP, CX4, 10GBase-T (bay 2) or stacking modules (bay 1). It is ideal for uplinks to a 10Gb core or for direct connections to 10Gb servers or storage.
Dell Networking 6248 Switch
The Dell Networking 6248GbE Layer 3 switch offers significant rack density and advanced core switching capabilities for enterprise networks. It includes 48 RJ-45 ports, 4 combo SFP ports and two rear-panel bays with support for optional 10Gb XFP, CX4, 10GBase-T (bay 2) or stacking modules (bay 1). It is ideal for uplinks to a 10Gb core or for direct connections to 10Gb servers or storage.
Dell Networking 6248P Switch
The Dell Networking 6248PGbE Layer 3 switch offers significant rack density, advanced switching and power over Ethernet (PoE) convenience for power-dependent network devices. It features 48 RJ-45 PoE ports*, 4 combo SFP ports and two rear-panel bays with support for optional 10Gb XFP, CX4, 10GBase-T (bay 2) or stacking modules (bay 1). It is ideal for uplinks to a 10Gb core or for direct connections to 10Gb servers or storage.
