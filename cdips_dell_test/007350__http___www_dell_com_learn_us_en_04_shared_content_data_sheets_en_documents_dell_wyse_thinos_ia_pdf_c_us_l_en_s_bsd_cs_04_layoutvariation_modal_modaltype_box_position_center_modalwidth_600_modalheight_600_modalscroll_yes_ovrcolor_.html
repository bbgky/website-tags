This brochure describes the key component of Dell desktop virtualization product offerings and one of the most secure operating systems in the world for cloud and virtual computing environments. Wyse ThinOS is certified with Citrix®, Microsoft®, VMware®, Wyse vWorkspace and other ecosystem partners.

Wyse ThinOS is available across a wide range of hardware form factors to suit your budget, application performance, and environmental needs–from highly affordable to high performance. The line-up includes the Wyse 3010, the Wyse 3020, the Wyse 5010 thin clients and the Wyse 5000 series all-in-one thin clients.
