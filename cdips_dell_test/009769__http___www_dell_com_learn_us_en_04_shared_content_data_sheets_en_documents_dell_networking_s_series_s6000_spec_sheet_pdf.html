Dell Networking S6000 Spec Sheet

  • Print
  • Check out this page on Dell.com! Email
  • Download PDF (159k)
View the full article as a PDF >
High-density 40GbE switch (32 ports of 40GbE or 104 x 10GbE or 96 x 10GbE and 8 x 40GbE) with high performance for ToR, MoR and EoR deployments. The S6000 includes feature-rich Dell Networking OS9, VLT, and built-in network virtualization features with support for Dell Open Automation Framework
