Improve clinician efficiency, secure patient data and increase IT productivity

Healthcare is a dynamic industry that faces constant demand to innovate to improve patient care and enable greater clinician productivity. This challenge is compounded with the responsibility to protect patient privacy while providing clinicians with quick access to the essential data they need to make crucial decisions. How can your IT team leverage technology to help increase productivity, secure critical data and improve overall patient care, while simplifying IT systems and processes? 

Built on top of Dell Cloud Client Computing technology, Dell Mobile Clinical Computing (MCC) is a fully integrated and validated solution designed to meet the challenges of data security, clinical efficiency and IT productivity in healthcare organizations. MCC provides clinicians with secure and easy access to patient information anytime, anywhere. At the same time, MCC enables IT to simplify management of desktops and applications, resulting in fewer help desk calls, better service delivery and higher clinician satisfaction. 

MCC enables clinical staff to roam a session from one device to another with fast, secure access to data and applications. Combining desktop virtualization, strong authentication technologies integrated with single sign-on (SSO) and optimized endpoint devices into a single solution, MCC stores critical data centrally while securing endpoints, helping reduce the risk of lost or stolen data and simplifying HIPAA compliance.

Why Dell for Mobile Clinical Computing?  

Dell Mobile Clinical Computing (MCC) isn’t just a configuration of technologies; it’s a solution development and consulting practice at Dell that is led by IT professionals with years of experience in healthcare consulting, implementation and support services. With our in-depth understanding of clinician requirements, patient data management and IT challenges, we can tailor a solution that uniquely addresses the needs of your organization. 

Experience the advantages of working with Dell for clinical computing:
  • Deep domain expertise — Designed and delivered by healthcare IT professionals
  • Validated solution stacks — Technology rigorously tested in Dell labs for compatibility, scalability and availability
  • Proven Dell Cloud Client Computing technology — Based on Dell Cloud Client Computing technology, MCC is an integrated portfolio of the latest virtualization and management technologies, end-to-end infrastructure products, endpoint products, software and services used by organizations worldwide
  • Flexible delivery models — Delivered to you according to your specific needs and goals: business-ready configurations, build-and-transfer or as as-a-service
  • Fully supported solution — Certified and trained Dell Services and Support professionals collaborate with key technology partners to provide you end-to-end solution support
  • Single point of accountability — Dell-led from solution design to implementation and support, resulting in a simplified process with more accountability and fewer contracts
See how the Dell MCC solution can significantly benefit your organization:
  • Improve clinical efficiency – Enable quicker clinician access to records anytime, anywhere through capabilities such as single sign-on, fast login, application auto-launch, location awareness, session transfer and follow-me printing
  • Enhance data security – Lower the risk of data breaches and streamline HIPAA compliance by storing information centrally within the data center, not the endpoint, and ensuring access to that information through role-based information delivery and multifactor authentication
  • Increase IT productivity – Standardize and simplify IT processes for reduced downtime, faster issue resolution, efficient patch management and dynamic provisioning of user applications and data, thereby reducing help-desk calls and freeing IT staff to focus on more strategic tasks
  • Improve patient care – Create better patient impressions, costs and outcomes through shorter length of stay, reduction in duplicate tests and faster decision-making and time-to-care
