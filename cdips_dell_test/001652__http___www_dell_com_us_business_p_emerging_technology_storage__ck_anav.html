The you-defined data center

For businesses that are seeking to build a future-ready data center, Dell supports the strategy of implementing virtualization and software-defined storage gradually—incrementally. We value the balance between traditional storage and new technologies and recognize that some businesses are expanding their existing SAN and NAS infrastructure while others are deploying software-defined storage (SDS) solutions with hypervisor-integrated systems and open source solutions. Dell and its partners enable you to choose the best approach for your datacenter and evolve at a pace that meets your business needs. With this generation of Dell’s software-defined solutions, we’re giving you the freedom to choose what works best for your workloads and what will bring superior efficiency,reliability and scalability to your data center.

Dell Scality Object Storage Solutions

Scality and Dell offer a solution that combines Scality RING software with Dell server and storage hardware for capacity-driven storage environments. The Dell Scality solution provides an object-based architecture that scales to multiple petabytes and billions of objects with linear performance scaling. These software-based solutions are designed for 100% uptime and run on standard x86 servers to increase flexibility and bring cloud economics to large storage environments. Take advantage of these benefits:

• Parallel, high-performance access for mixed workloads and multiple applications
• Flexible data protection (erasure coding, replication, geo)
• Multi-protocol data delivery and access (shared-namespace file and object storage plus optional OpenStack™ APIs)
• Single-site or multi-geo configurations
• Predictable storage expenditure for future growth
• Global service and support from Dell and Scality
