Microsoft Cloud Platform System Powered by Dell

 
Accelerate your journey to the cloud

Increase the speed, ease and assurance of your cloud deployments while realizing cost savings with Microsoft Cloud Platform System (CPS) Powered by Dell.

CPS introduces a validated, ready-to-run, optimized solution that features Microsoft software strategically coupled with Dell PowerEdge servers, Dell Storage and Dell Networking in an integrated, cost-effective appliance.

Built upon the foundation of their long-standing, innovative relationship, Microsoft and Dell now offer this infrastructure and automation model to deliver standardized cloud solutions. Dell is the only development and launch partner for CPS.

Optimize application delivery

Drive savings while ensuring performance. Optimize delivery of your workloads with Dell systems designed for cloud demands and smarter resource utilization.

Designed to reduce initial CapEx, CPS features a cutting-edge software-defined storage solution leveraging Dell PowerEdge servers, Dell Storage and Microsoft Storage Spaces to deliver over 1 PB of data storage, with affordable, space-saving density.*  

Your OpEx can be lower with CPS, too. Energy-efficient CPS infrastructure provides high-performance computing needed for cloud while saving up to 38% in energy costs.*  Integrated systems management further reduces OpEx by simplifying processes and requiring fewer manual steps by IT staff.

 
 Deliver service-centric IT to accelerate responsiveness

Innovate faster and increase agility with dynamic capabilities that save time and improve operational effectiveness. Quickly procure and easily deploy validated, pre-configured CPS solutions to meet your ever-changing business demands. Rest assured the same infrastructure and automation model will be in effect wherever you use CPS, reducing confusion and delays that can arise from multiple locations, configurations or processes.

Once deployed, OpenManage and Active Fabric Manager make completing complex tasks — like detecting and adjusting for network fabric configuration changes — simple and consistent. This way, you can focus your IT staff on innovation and serving customers, rather than operating and troubleshooting.

Empower users and customers

CPS helps you ensure a positive user and customer experience without administrative disruption, with features for self-service provisioning, data governance and regulatory compliance.

In addition, CPS delivers a consistent, high-quality cloud experience. Powered by cloud-proven Dell platforms, CPS delivers the assurance of the high availability, resiliency and reliability you expect from trusted cloud solution providers Microsoft and Dell. CPS includes infrastructure automation tools that help you maximize service uptime and minimize manual, error-prone tasks.
 
Should issues occur, resolve them easily with a single trusted provider. Eliminate the hassle and coordination of troubleshooting across multiple vendors so you can focus on advancing your business.
