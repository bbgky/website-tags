Protect against diverse threats without adding complexity

Deliver better protection than what is offered by consumer-grade products for your small business, retail store, government agency, remote sites or branch offices, with Dell™ unified threat management solutions. Provide robust protection while maintaining high network performance and simplifying management. Deliver gateway antivirus, anti-malware, anti-spam, intrusion prevention, content/URL filtering, SSL VPN and application control capabilities, without adding significant costs or complexity.
 

Comprehensive security

Implement advanced unified threat management by combining intrusion prevention, gateway antivirus, anti-spyware, content/URL filtering, enforced client antivirus, and anti-spam services. Application intelligence and control capabilities help ensure that bandwidth is available for business-critical applications, while throttling or blocking unproductive applications.
  • SonicWALL TZ Series

High-performance protection

Protect your organizations from a wide range of threats without slowing down the network. Examine traffic simultaneously across all ports, without introducing latency, using deep packet inspection technology.  
  • SonicWALL NSA Series
  • SonicWALL TZ Series

Remote access and wireless connectivity

Provide native virtual private network (VPN) access for devices running on Apple® iOS, Google® Android™, Microsoft® Windows®, Mac OS, and Linux® operating systems. Decontaminate threats from VPN traffic with Clean VPN™ technology. Encrypt traffic while helping to ensure the integrity of all traffic passing through with clean wireless, which combines next-generation firewalls with high-speed wireless.  
  • SonicWALL NSA Series
  • SonicWALL TZ Series
  • SonicWALL Clean Wireless

Security management

You can easily use and understand Dell unified threat management solutions, as they are built on an advanced, object-based management SonicOS operating system. Experience enterprise-class, high-performance protection, at a lower total cost of ownership for your distributed enterprise or small-to-midsized business.
