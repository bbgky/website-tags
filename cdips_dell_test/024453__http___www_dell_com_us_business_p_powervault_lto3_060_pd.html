LTO-3-060 Tape Backup Drive

  • Single external Linear Tape-Open Ultrium 3 (LTO-3) half height tape drive, internal model available within server configurations
  • High performance and capacity that back up a maximum of 400 GB* (native) of information at up to 216 GB/hr (native)
  • Backward read-and-write compatible with Linear Tape-Open Ultrium 2 (LTO-2) media and read-compatible with Linear Tape-Open Ultrium 1 (LTO-1) media
  • Available with LTO-3 (write-once, read-many) WORM  media to protect your data from being altered or changed

Tape Backup Technology
