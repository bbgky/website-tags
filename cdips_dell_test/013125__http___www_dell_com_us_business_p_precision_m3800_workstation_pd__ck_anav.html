Incredible clarity, anywhere you take it.
The world's thinnest and lightest 15" Workstation: Large projects are easier than ever to carry anywhere. The Precision M3800 is super thin and lightweight, yet offers full-blown Workstation performance — all packed into a carbon fiber and aluminum design you'll be proud to carry.

 

New 4K Ultra HD display: View, create and edit your work in perfect clarity on the new, optional 4K Ultra HD display that offers a whole new level of eye-popping detail with over 8 million pixels — that’s three million more pixels than a Retina display. Beauty meets strength in the sturdy Corning® Gorilla® NBT™ glass screen.

 

Intuitive touch navigation: Pinch, drag and swipe your way through applications like Adobe Creative Cloud with 10-finger touch capability that enables a new, immersive way to interact with your Workstation. Touch displays help you create and present in imaginative ways.

 

Easy integration into your office: When you're back at your desk, multitasking can be a breeze with support for up to three external monitors. Need to transfer large files? Data transfers are quick and easy with new Thunderbolt 2.0 connectivity.

Outstanding professional performance
Accelerated workflows: 4th gen Intel® Core™ i7 processor provide the power to extract maximum performance from the demanding professional software you rely on to do your job.

Professional graphics: From rendering to complex modeling, you'll notice an incredible performance boost thanks to NVIDIA® Quadro K1100M graphics with 2GB* memory.

 

Big storage for big jobs: Easily store, transfer and access large and complex 3D, multimedia and video project files with the speed and reliability of up to 2TB of solid state drive technology.

Reliable and secure
Independent Software Vendor (ISV) certification: The Precision Workstations you purchase are tested to ensure the high-performance applications you rely on every day run smoothly. ISV certification is the result of precise engineering and rigorous testing to optimize performance and reliability to run leading industry applications for engineering, manufacturing, media, entertainment, healthcare, life sciences, oil, gas, economics and financial services. Dell ISV-certifications cover the most popular independent software applications including:
  • Dassault Systèmes including CATIA, and SolidWorks
  • Autodesk Product Design Suite including Inventor® and AutoCAD, 3D CAD and Revit®.
  • Adobe Creative Cloud® including Premiere Pro CC and After Effects® CC
For a complete list of ISV certifications, click here.

 

Network safely: Access remote networks confidently knowing that Dell Data Protection | Encryption is there to encrypt your data and protect you from malware across all endpoints, including external media and public cloud storage.

 

Carry confidently: The high-end materials used to build the M3800 weren't chosen just for their beauty. The Corning® Gorilla® Glass NBT™ display, machined aluminum frame and strong carbon-fiber base provide industrial-grade durability.
