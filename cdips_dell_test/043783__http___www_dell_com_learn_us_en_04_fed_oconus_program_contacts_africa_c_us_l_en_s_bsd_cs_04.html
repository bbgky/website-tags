OCONUS Program Telephone Numbers — Africa

Dell™ provides international phone numbers from multiple countries that is available 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, for the life of your Dell system. These numbers route to a North America Technical Support queue that is managed by English speaking technicians. There is no charge for each call, and no limit is placed on the number of calls you can make to Dell. Dell representatives can resolve 70–80% of customer issues over the phone.
