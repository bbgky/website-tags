Philips Healthcare partners with Dell OEM to deliver their virtualized PACS solution

View the full article as a PDF >

Dell is proactive and explains in detail what it plans to do to solve the problem and how long it will take. Plus, it always follows up to make sure we’re satisfied with the resolution, which is something we’ve not experienced with other partners.

Philips Healthcare, The Netherlands
Challenge
Philips Healthcare needed a scalable storage platform for its customer – the CHRU de Lille hospital – and its IntelliSpace PACS, to reduce space, energy and cooling costs, while enhancing access to high-quality radiology images.
Solution
The firm worked with Dell OEM Solutions to create a virtualized storage environment for its IntelliSpace PACS using Dell EqualLogic storage and Dell ProSupport with Mission Critical.
Benefits
• Images are quickly accessed in intelligent storage environment
• Power and cooling costs are significantly reduced through virtualization
• Technical help and advice is fast and transparent with Dell ProSupport
• Radiology images are protected by reliable Dell storage
• Ongoing storage costs are reduced with efficient tiering
• Less rack space is required because storage resources are consolidated
