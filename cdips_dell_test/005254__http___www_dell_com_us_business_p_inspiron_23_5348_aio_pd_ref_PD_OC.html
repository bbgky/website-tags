Responsive and expansive display
High def view: This expansive 23" Full HD (1080p) display provides the perfect viewing for all your favorite photos and videos.

Stunning edge-to-edge touch: With the 10-finger capacitive touch screen option, your every tap, swipe and scroll is responsive and accurate. Unlike optical touch, which uses cameras in the bezel area to track movement, capacitive touch has sensors under the glass to assure movements are more fluid. Capacitive touch combines with a sleek edge-to-edge display to make your Windows 8.1 experience even more amazing.

Powerfully connected
Faster everything: Balance your checkbook, post to social media, upload a movie and listen to music at the same time — with less lag — thanks to the impressive speed and power of up to 4th Gen Intel® Core™ i7 processors.

Quick response: Games, videos and photo editing all get a boost with up to 1GB* Intel HD graphics performance.

Easy connections: Connect to your phone, printer, scanner and other devices with two USB 3.0 and five USB 2.0 ports, plus an HDMI In and HDMI Out port to connect to a second display or TV monitor.
