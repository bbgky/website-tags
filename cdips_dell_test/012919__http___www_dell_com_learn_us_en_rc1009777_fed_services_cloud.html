Federal Cloud Services

Access virtually unlimited computing capacity and respond to changing agency demands


Your operations have grown more complex, and you’re ready to move from legacy architectures to cloud-based platforms. A key enabler of IT transformation and innovation, the cloud can go to work for you in every environment, application, or mission-specific function. But how can you find the right cloud solution?

Look no further than Dell Cloud for U.S. Government (DCG). If you need to obtain a federal Authority to Operate (ATO) cloud to host applications and databases with specific performance, privacy or compliance requirements, there’s a DCG solution for your needs. And there’s a way to migrate everyday office applications, data and workloads to a FedRAMP-compliant cloud.

DCG is delivered as an Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) offering using a multi-tenant community cloud computing environment. It is available to U.S federal, tribal, state, and local government customers, U.S. higher education, U.S. government contractors, and Federally Funded Research and Development Centers (FFRDCs) organizations.

This readily scalable, on-demand service gives you the flexibility and control to meet fast-changing workload. Built to federal NIST 800-53 and ATO security standards to support FedRAMP, FISMA and DIACAP requirements, it can host your sensitive workloads in one of our data centers or your designated site. Find out why DCG is the cloud solution you’ve been looking for.

For more information, contact your Dell Services Representative or call 703-289-3545
 

Solutions

     
Gain efficiency and simplicity through our expertise and seamless approach to NIST-dedicated and FedRAMP clouds.
   

FedRAMP Compliant Cloud
A FedRAMP-authorized multi-tenant cloud designed for government agencies needing to move FedRAMP, FISMA and DIACAP workloads seamlessly to a federally compliant and secure cloud environment. DCG received FedRAMP Provisional Authorization to Operate (P-ATO) on April 21st, 2016.		 NIST Compliant Cloud
Our dedicated FISMA/DIACAP cloud solution includes the same features and functionality as our multi-tenant (FedRAMP) cloud with the additional peace of mind that comes with an isolated/dedicated infrastructure.  
 Dell Cloud Consulting Services
We work with you to guide your agency’s creation of a cloud strategy that matches your mission objectives, without introducing complexity. Get the support you need through every phase – regardless of which cloud vendors you use.

 Managed Cloud Services
We understand the rigorous standards your agency must meet, and we can help you navigate the process. Get help in obtaining a federal Authority to Operate (ATO) cloud or move to a Fed RAMP cloud.
