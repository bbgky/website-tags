Secure. Manageable. Reliable. Latitude.
Best-in-class security: Only Dell offers comprehensive encryption, advanced authentication and leading-edge malware prevention from a single source. Dell Data Protection | Encryption protects your data across all endpoints including external media, self-encrypting drives, and in public cloud storage such as Dropbox, Box and OneDrive. DDP | Security Tools offers advanced authentication options to help you keep unauthorized users out of your data. Dell takes a new approach to malware prevention by protecting your data and users from all untrusted content with DDP | Protected Workspace.

Best-in-class manageability: Dell builds management capabilities into our Latitude portfolio to make the most of your hardware investment. With the new Dell Client Command Suite, our free automation tools help simplify IT time spent on deploying, monitoring and updating systems. We also offer five generations of updates, and with Latitude’s single-door access, you can easily upgrade internal components.

Outstanding reliability so you can rest easy: More durable than its predecessor, and part of the most durable laptop family, the new Latitude 15 3000 Series features a durable, built-to-last chassis that has undergone highly accelerated life testing and is backed by enterprise-class support and services, including optional Dell ProSupport.
