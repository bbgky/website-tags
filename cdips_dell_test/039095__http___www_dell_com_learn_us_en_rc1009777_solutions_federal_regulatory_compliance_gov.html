Federal procurement: contracts, certifications and compliance

We understand the complex procurement process for federal agencies — you’re required to operate within the strict confines of purchasing contracts, regulatory compliance and certifications. Use the following links to find out if your anticipated purchase of Dell IT solutions and services meets the specific guidelines for your agency.

Contract Availability and Pricing
Access your online store and find federal purchase contract information. Our services and our software and hardware products are on a number of federal purchasing schedules, including Government-wide Acquisition Contracts (GWACs).

Log in to Premier
Log into your Premier account portal to access your standardized product configurations. If your agency does not have a Premier account portal, you can learn more about Premier accounts and how to set up this streamlined procurement process.

Federal Regulatory Compliance Information
We place a strong emphasis on creating environmentally friendly products and processes. Learn how we comply with or exceed federal and international environmental regulations.
