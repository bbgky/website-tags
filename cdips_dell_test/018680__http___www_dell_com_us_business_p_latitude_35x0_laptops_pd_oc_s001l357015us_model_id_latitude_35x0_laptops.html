Features & Design

Designed with Windows 10 Pro in mind – fast, smooth, more secure.
Return to Top
Essential for business. Perfect for you.
Stylish and powerful: The sophisticated new Latitude 15 3000 Series features your choice of 5th or 6th Gen Intel® Core™ processors, plus Windows 10 Pro for power and productivity.

 

Vivid view: Enjoy your 15.6 " display with an HD or Full HD anti-glare display or an optional HD touch screen. For a higher-quality visual experience, choose 2GB of optional discrete graphics.

 

Power on the go: Work from anywhere with all-day battery life. And with the easy access bottom latch, replace your battery on the go with no tools required.

 

Plenty of ports: Modern performance is never far away with all the ports you need, including USB 3.0 and 2.0, RJ45, HDMI and VGA ports.

Store more: Get up to 16GB of memory and 1TB of HDD or hybrid storage options for all your important data.

Be recognized: Using Windows Hello with Dell’s infrared camera forgetting your password will no longer interrupt your day. (available on touch-enable configurations only)
Return to Top
Secure. Manageable. Reliable. Latitude.
Best-in-class security: Only Dell offers comprehensive encryption, advanced authentication, and leading-edge malware prevention from a single source. Dell Data Protection | Encryption protects your data across all endpoints, external media, and public cloud storage. One-touch, preset templates enable compliance right out of the box allowing you to avoid costly fines if a device is compromised, lost, or stolen. Dell Data Protection | Security Tools offers advanced authentication options to help keep unauthorized users out of your data. Dell Data Protection | Protected Workspace takes a new approach to malware prevention by guarding you and your data from all suspicious content. If an attack occurs, the malware is sealed off and a safe environment is restored in only 20 seconds.

 

World’s most manageable: Full Dell management capabilities to allow you to make the most of your hardware investment with free Dell Client Command Suite tools to allow flexible and automated BIOS or system configurations. Factory Installed with Dell Command | Update allowing users to easily identify and download updates. Dell Command | Power Manager is also factory installed for easy customization of battery power options, plus with Battery Extender Mode users can prolong battery life with one-click, reducing energy-intense hardware processes. Dell KACE System Management solutions are also available for those organizations needing a comprehensive management console.

 

Outstanding reliability and design: The new Latitude 15 3000 Series features a durable, built-to-last chassis that has undergone extensive military-grade MIL-STD 810G testing plus Dell H.A.L.T. testing- our Highly Accelerated Life Cycle Testing regimen ensures that your system can withstand real-world conditions.
