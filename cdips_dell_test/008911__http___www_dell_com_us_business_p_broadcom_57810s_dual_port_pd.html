Get the most from your PowerEdge investment

Get maximum performance from your PowerEdge™ servers with Broadcom® 57810S Dual-Port 10GbE KR Blade Converged Mezzanine Cards. Each Broadcom 57810S offers massive bandwidth through two 10GbE ports and is supported by the established performance of the Broadcom architecture and software stack.

 

  • Easy integration: Developed to the latest IEEE standards and offering PCI hot plug/hot swap capability, the Broadcom 57810S fits easily into your virtualized or workload-intensive PowerEdge server environment.   • Improved management efficiency: Reach new levels of speed and convenience through remote access to Dell’s Lifecycle Controller management framework.
  • Advanced capabilities: Bring scalable performance and flexible provisioning to your data center through high-performance hardware offload (iSCSI and FCoE-ready) and switch-independent NIC partitioning (NPAR) capabilities.

Advanced Features

  • Support for:

    VMware™ NetQueue and Microsoft™ VMQ - Helps to increase performance in virtual server environments
    Switch independent NIC partitioning (NPAR) - Enables segmentation of a single 10GbE port into as many as four partitions, allowing dynamic allocation of bandwidth on as needed basis to improve application performance.

    High performance hardware offload for iSCSI and FCoE-ready storage protocols

    VLAN support (IEEE 802.1q VLAN tagging) - Helps to improve management efficiency, security and network flexibility

    Precision time protocol (PTP) ready - Provides more granular level of time synchronization between the constituents of an Ethernet network

    MSI-X - Enhances overall system performance, reduce system overhead, lower interrupt latency and increase host CPU utilization

    Broadcom Advanced Control Suite (BACS) management application - Streamline management tasks, optimize performance and improve up-time

    iSCSI remote boot support - Helps IT administrators significantly reduce time to deployment of new or replacement servers.
