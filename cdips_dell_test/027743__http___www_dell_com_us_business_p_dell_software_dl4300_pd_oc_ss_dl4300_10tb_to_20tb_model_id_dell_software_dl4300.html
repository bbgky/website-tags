Optimize efficiency


Deploy the turnkey DL4300 quickly to maximize efficiency for your growing business, and curb expansive storage growth.
  • Streamline and accelerate backup operations to minimize backup windows.
  • Shrink your backup footprint with integrated, inline block-level deduplication and compression, combined with optimized backups for WAN replication.
  • Recover granular data with the ability to take snapshots as often as every five minutes.
  • Minimize hardware sizing, backup software configuration and system testing.
  • Alleviate appliance management headaches with the simplified AppAssure Central Management Console.
  • Provision DL4300 storage automatically with the AppAssure Appliance Configuration Wizard

Ensure business resiliency


Take advantage of advanced, integrated, technologies that form the foundation of AppAssure software for Microsoft® Windows® and Linux®.
  • Establish virtual standby machines that can be activated quickly to restore business-critical servers.
  • Take advantage of near-instant recovery for protected virtual machines or servers with Live Recovery. You can retrieve an impacted server’s application and data directly from the backup image in the event of an outage, keeping users productive and helping you meet tight RTOs and RPOs.
  • Test and verify recovery of backups for Microsoft Exchange and SQL Server automatically with Verified Recovery. Identify data corruption early and prevent corrupted data blocks from being transferred during backup.
  • Restore backups from physical to virtual, virtual to virtual, virtual to physical, or physical to cloud with Universal Recovery. In addition, this feature allows you to carry out bare metal restores to dissimilar hardware.
  • Create a defined restore point for the appliance, or recover the appliance back to factory settings, with Rapid Appliance Self Recovery or RASR. 
  • Collect hardware, storage and operating system information, as well as a single management tab within the appliance, to manage standby VMs with new diagnostic tools such as Windows Snipping tool and enhanced support tools such as Dell DSET.

Safeguard your data and applications from unexpected failures, and have the assurance of knowing you’ll be able to get back to business in minutes. The Dell DL4300 Backup and Recovery appliance makes it possible.

Fully configured with AppAssure software, the DL4300 is a robust, highly-scalable backup appliance that helps you protect the virtual servers critical to your business, whether they’re at your home or branch office, data center or private cloud.

The DL4300 is housed in a 2U appliance, and is available in two configurations. Choose the Standard edition to protect between 5TB and 60TB of storage. The High Capacity edition protects between 40TB and 120TB. The DL4300 can be expanded with up to four PowerVault MD1400 expansion shelves. In addition, the DL4300 provides up to four standby virtual machines with snapshot, replication, deduplication and compression software.

*The DL4300 Standard Edition can scale to support up to 60TB of licensed capacity. For Capacities needed beyond 60TB please contact your Dell Sales Team.
