Dell and Citrix — Global Alliance Partners

Working together to empower your mobile workforce

The rapid growth in portable devices has led to changing business requirements, expanding teams of remote workers and an increased need for data storage. The Dell and Citrix global alliance allows customers to support end users anytime, anywhere, on any computing device. The freedom of mobility increases flexibility, productivity and performance across your organization.

From the data center to the endpoint device, our jointly developed solutions integrate features and functionality from both the Dell and Citrix portfolios and deliver integrated solution stacks for fast, standardized deployment. Dell cloud client computing solutions for Citrix are designed from the ground up to help you address complex challenges not met by traditional client deployments to empower your mobile workforce.
