Features & Design

Available with Windows 8.1 – designed for a smooth, versatile PC experience.
Return to Top
Made for movement
The XPS™ 18 is designed to move. Unplug it and take it with you to create your own setup anywhere. Stream your favorite tunes, review reports at your office or connect with friends on the couch.
Such a lightweight:
At just 2.2kg,* this baby is built for travel. Carry it easily from room to room or enjoy it as a tablet comfortably in your lap because it’s the world’s thinnest and lightest portable all-in-one.
Powered for hours:
Spend more time away from the outlet with excellent battery life that gives you 5 hours of running time.*
Two deft feet:
Built-in flip-out feet extend to keep the XPS 18 upright. It’s a snap to set up anywhere at a comfortable angle for playing games, creating presentations or watching your shows the way you want.
Return to Top
A touch of class
Experience sharing in a whole new way. Watch your favorite videos, play the latest games side by side, zoom in on spreadsheets or videoconference with coworkers on a large interactive touch screen. And do it all anywhere you want.

Fluid navigation: Tap, scroll, pinch and swipe your way around the web using two or more fingers to quickly navigate the net on the XPS 18’s responsive multi-touch screen.

Easy on the eyes: An 18.4" Full HD (1080p) touch screen provides plenty of space for streaming content or sharing photos. And the LED backlit display boasts IPS/TrueLife and FHD resolution (1920x1080) for va-va-voom-viewing, so when it’s time to get down to business, the extra large screen lets you can leave the projector behind.

Stand-up guise: Plug in to a power source with an exterior docking stand. The versatile XPS 18 converts easily from a tablet to a desktop with the adjustable stand so that you can decide where and how you operate. Dock it on your desk to check email or stand it upright in the kitchen to display recipes.
