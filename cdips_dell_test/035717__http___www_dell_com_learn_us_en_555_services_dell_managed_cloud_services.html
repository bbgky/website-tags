    Expert management for cloud operating systems and applications

    Ensure operating system and application availability, performance and reliability on your cloud platforms with IT Infrastructure Library-based (ITIL) practices.

    Trust Dell to manage your cloud operating systems and applications.
    Innovative cloud solutions are revolutionizing the business world, but they can also create new IT challenges. Even small cloud implementations need to be monitored and managed to maintain their health and security.

    Let us do that for you. When you engage Dell experts to manage your clouds, you also get access to a rich network of cloud partner solutions and Dell vertical market experts.

    Use Dell Managed Cloud Services to manage:
    • Multiple cloud platforms across your entire business.
    • MySQL, Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle Server, Hadoop, Mongo and other databases
    • Microsoft IIS, Oracle Weblogic, TomCat, Apache, JBoss and other web servers
    • Windows and Linux operating systems
    • Microsoft SharePoint, Microsoft Exchange, Microsoft Active Directory and many more enterprise applications
    Dell Managed Cloud Services includes:
    • A customer delivery executive who serves as your single point of accountability
    • ITIL-based Service and Operations Management, a standards-based framework for incident and problem management, monitoring and alerting, and change management —delivered by Dell or by a Dell self-service tool
    • 24/7 intelligent voice-response support, available through a toll-free number
    • A self-service, portal that delivers IT operations lifecycle management capabilities
    Contact a Dell representative to start planning for your cloud solution.
