Sleek and powerful

The Wyse 5050 All-in-One (AIO) PCoIP zero client combines the security and performance of the Wyse 5030 PCoIP zero client for VMware with the elegant design of Dell’s best-selling P24 LED monitor. The Wyse 5050 AIO provides a best-in-class virtual experience with superior manageability – at a better value than purchasing a zero client and high resolution monitor separately.


A dedicated hardware PCoIP engine delivers the highest level of display performance available for advanced applications, including CAD, 3D solids modeling, video editing and more. Elegant in appearance and energy efficient, the Wyse 5050 AIO is a fully functional VMware View endpoint that delivers a true PC-like experience. It offers the full benefits of an efficient and secure centralized computing environment, like rich multimedia, high-resolution 3D graphics, HD media, and full USB peripheral interoperability locally (LAN) or remotely (WAN).
Easy to deploy
With a simple one-cord design and automatic setup out of the box, the Wyse 5050 AIO is simple to install and use. The zero client features its own integrated 23.6” high-resolution monitor, reducing clutter, occupied space and wires. Wyse Device Manager allows hands-off management, either on-premise or cloud-based. 


High security with PCoIP
Wyse zero clients are extremely secure with zero attack surface for viruses and malware. The PCoIP protocol transfers images only, in the form of pixel location information, so your sensitive information never leaves the data center. All software lies safely inside central systems. The PCoIP security module uses the AES 256 and NSA Suite B cyphers, which meet government-level security requirements.


Best-in-class design
The sleek design and built-in power supply of the Wyse 5050 AIO create an exceptionally small footprint. The integrated monitor with 1080p resolution allows you to maximize productivity without cluttering your workspace. Six USB ports (4 on the side of the display) along with a DVI and VGA port allow convenient access and dual display capability, and you can position the device however you’d like with the rotating and detachable base.

Get end-to-end support 24x7.

For end-to-end support throughout the Wyse thin client lifecycle, Dell offers Deployment Services, ProSupport and Accidental Damage Service. This services portfolio helps you streamline deployment and support so you can focus on innovating and growing your business.

With Dell Deployment, you get fast, accurate deployment by Dell specialists who set up your onsite IT infrastructure for you. Dell ProSupport offers prompt, expert IT support 24x7 at a consistent cost. Accidental Damage Service is insurance for your hardware. Ideal for high-risk environments, it reduces downtime and covers drops, spills and electrical surges.
