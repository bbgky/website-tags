    Dell SonicWALL Firewall

    What does a firewall do?

    Good firewalls protect against viruses, spam, spyware, and other
    threats; they protect the network by not letting the bad stuff in.
    Plus, they give you the power to control web traffic and applications
    used within the network helping it to operate more efficiently.

    How do you know when you need a firewall?

    All networks should have a firewall, but a next-generation firewall is crucial if:
    • Your company collects credit card information. Dell SonicWALL firewalls
      comply with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DSS).
    • Your company partners with larger companies. Your small business must
      have at minimum the same level of security as the larger company.
    • Your company has remote offices, remote workers, wireless access,
      or if your employees use smart phones, tablet and other mobile devices.
      Dell SonicWALL will keep a secure network perimeter.

    Dell Networking

    What does a switch do?
    A switch enables your employees to access your network and all of the devices connected to it.
    Ie: Printers, phones, PCs etc.

    How do you know when you need it?
    When your business has outgrown your internet providers switch. As your business grows you
    may not have enough ports to connect all of your new employees to your network and devices.

    What is the difference between web managed and fully managed?
    • Unmanaged switches are plug-and-play ready, enabling users and peripherals to be networked.
    • Web managed configuration enables a web interface for light configuration (segment users into
      VLANs for example) via a front-facing push-button.
    • Fully managed allows use of the command-line interface (CLI) for granular & complex configurations.

    Every Dell Network switch is backed by a limited-lifetime hardware warranty.
