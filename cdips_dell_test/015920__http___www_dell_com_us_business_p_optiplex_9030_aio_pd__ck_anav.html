Features & Design

The right fit. The right features.
Robust performance: The OptiPlex 9030 All-in-One provides performance features to power your organization’s productivity. Equipped with up to a 4th Gen Intel ® Core ™ i7 processor and Intel® HD Graphics 4600, the OptiPlex 9030 All-in-One seamlessly handles intensive tasks. A stunning combination of appearance and performance, this all-in-one offers the perfect solution for reception areas, self-service check-in, conference rooms, kiosks and graphic-rich medical tasks. Take advantage of optional next generation discrete graphics for an even better performance.

Impressive versatile display: Watch Windows 8.1 come to life through a vibrant 23" display with edge-to-edge glass. Add a dual monitor for effortless multi-tasking. Built-in VESA mounting capability provides space-saving versatility for your workspace.

Collaborative features: Your OptiPlex 9030 All-in-One is Microsoft® Lync® Certified for clear, reliable communication across your network. Transfer data quickly with six high-speed USB 3.0 ports. Connect easily to external monitors using DisplayPort 1.2a or HDMI connections.

IT efficiency in an elegant package
An attractive fit: Dell OptiPlex 9030 All-in-One is Dell’s thinnest all-in-one in the market today, and offers a standout stylish and streamlined design ideal for highly visible workspaces. Deploying the OptiPlex 9030 All-in-One is as easy as lifting the lid, attaching the stand and plugging it in. Ergonomic and clutter-free, the height adjustable stand of the OptiPlex 9030 All-in-One rotates for ultimate versatility, helping you work the way you need to.

IT efficiency: Engineered to save IT departments time and money, an innovative new chassis provides tool-less serviceability and simplified cabling, making this all-in-one simple to service and support.

Comprehensive security
Protect your most important asset: Best-in-class security and encryption solutions keep your data protected from your desktop to external media and to the cloud. Manage data encryption centrally across devices without disrupting end users or existing IT processes with Dell Data Protection. Tighten security with advanced authentication options and leading-edge malware prevention. 

Tools for a secure system: Stay in control of access to systems and information with Dell Data Protection | Security Tools for single sign-on and authentication management. Trusted Platform Module (TPM) grants an additional layer of data protection and secure credential storage. 

Protect your investment: Physically secure your system with lock slot support and lockable port cover. Chassis intrusion detection switch alerts you to any unauthorized access to the internal components of your OptiPlex systems.
